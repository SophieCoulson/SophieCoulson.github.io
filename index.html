Sophie Coulson

Sea Level, Ice Sheets, Mantle Dynamics
PhD Candidate, Harvard University

I'm a fifth year graduate student at Harvard University's Department of Earth and Planetary Sciences. My research focuses on flow and deformation in the Earth's interior and the effect this has on surface processes. Specifically, I use theory and numerical modelling to understand and predict the impact of mantle flow on topography and sea-level change. My work demonstrates that including mantle processes can dramatically alter the dynamics of regional and global climate systems. Between research, I teach undergraduate classes, and serve on my department's Diversity, Inclusion and Belonging Committee.
