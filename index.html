Sophie Coulson

Sea Level, Ice Sheets, Mantle Dynamics
PhD Candidate, Harvard University

I'm a fifth year graduate student at Harvard University's Department of Earth and Planetary Sciences. My research focuses on flow and deformation in the Earth's interior and the effect this has on surface processes. Between research, I teach undergraduate classes, and serve on my department's Diversity, Inclusion and Belonging Committee.
